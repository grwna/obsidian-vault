# Conditional Statement
$$
\begin{array}{l}
  P \to Q \\
  P \\
  \hline
  \therefore Q
\end{array}
$$
Using the logical connective $\to$.
The statement $P\to Q$ is equivalent to $P\lor\neg Q$, and these statements can be connected using the logical connectives. In that statement, $P$ is the *antecedent* and $Q$ is the *consequent*.
